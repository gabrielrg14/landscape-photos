Did you know?

The wonders of the world are separated into 2 parts: Ancient and Modern.
Such a separation occurred because, unfortunately, the vast majority of Ancient ones did not survive time.

More

Ancient

The seven traditional wonders are the Great Pyramid of Giza, the Colossus of Rhodes, the Lighthouse of Alexandria, the Mausoleum at Halicarnassus, the Temple of Artemis, the Statue of Zeus at Olympia, and the Hanging Gardens of Babylon.

Next

Modern

The seven modern wonders are The Great Wall of China, Petra, The Colosseum, Chichén Itzá, Machu Picchu, Taj Mahal, and Christ the Redeemer.
